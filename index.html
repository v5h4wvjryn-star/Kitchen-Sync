Frequently Asked Questions

How does the "Expiring Soon" system work? ↓

Kitchen Sync monitors your inventory items daily. It flags items like bread or muffins 3-7 days before their expiration date so you can plan meals around them.

Can I manage two houses? ↓

Yes! You can filter your inventory and grocery lists by location, such as "Home" vs. "Cabin", ensuring you never buy double for the wrong house.

Does the app support receipt scanning? ↓

Absolutely. Upload a photo or a file of your grocery receipt, and our OCR technology will automatically populate your pantry with items and prices.
